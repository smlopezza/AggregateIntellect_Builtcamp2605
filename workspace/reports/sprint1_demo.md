---

## Slide 2 — The problem I set out to validate

Latino newcomers arrive with the wrong mental model of networking.
My hypothesis: the gap is **tactical** — they don't know how to do it.

---

## Slide 3 — What I did

- 3 user interviews conducted **in Spanish**
- 2 Track B (successfully transitioned), 1 Track A (actively searching)
- Track B = retrospective; Track A = living the problem right now
- Mix of professional backgrounds: analytics/ML, data/AI, clinical healthcare

---

## Slide 4 — The insight that changed everything

My hypothesis was wrong.

> *"Everyone tells you the solution — cover letter, networking, LinkedIn. I can find all that online. What I need is to maintain motivation, because without that the rest doesn't matter."* — P3

The tactical information is **abundant**. What's missing is **emotional sustainability**.

---

## Slide 5 — What this means for the product

Before: *"networking coach"*
After: *"the thing that keeps you in the search"*

- Emotional load management → primary feature
- Tactical content (scripts, templates) → secondary
- Must meet users where they are: Track A users want to **avoid** networking, not improve at it
